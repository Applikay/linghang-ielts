Nowadays, we are producing more and more rubbish. Why do you think this is happening? What can governments do to help reduce the amount of rubbish produced?

Today, the production rate of total amount of landfill is increasing at an alarming rate. This trend will only continue if there are no measures taken to prevent it. In this essay, reasons behind the issue will be examined and possible solutions the reduction of rubbish will be discussed.

The prime reason for the increasing production of rubbish is the modern lifestyle. Firstly, in the the a modern society, convenience is highly important to everyone and it has led to high usage of single use products. For example, cutleries cutlery from take-away restaurants are thrown away after a single use. These goods make our lives easier, but at the same time generate more rubbish. Moreover, more waste is made through unnecessary purchases. These days, many people tend to purchase more than what they need and it often results in more rubbish. This point can be illustrated by the amount of food waste created by households on a daily basis. Many families often waste leftovers or even unused ingredients due to unnecessary shopping. Likewise, the modern lifestyle has led us to produce more waste than ever.

In addition, another reason for the landfill problem is the low awareness towards the seriousness of the problem. Many people do not feel responsible for the problem and merely seek convenience in their lives. For instance, some people do not recycle products that can be recycled due to irresponsibility and laziness.

However, there are some possible actions to resolve the problem. For one, governments can assist in raising people’s awareness through government campaigns and education. When the seriousness of the problem is known, it would be more likely for people to change and start taking actions. Further, introduction of new laws could be useful. For example, a restriction on how much landfill each household can produce could result in a reduction of the landfill.

In conclusion, modern lifestyles and our desire for more convenient lives have brought the about concerns with landfills. However, through assistance from governments, we can reduce the amount of waste and create a greener world.

Task Response: 6-7

Try to get an even balance between each of the two questions. You did not answer the question about the solution to the problem of too much rubbish as fully as the question about why the rate has increased.

Ideally you should have exactly two main points for each question in the form of two middle paragraphs.

As it is you really only mentioned that people should be made aware of the problem ( not a solution) and that laws could be adopted. You need to develop this more.
